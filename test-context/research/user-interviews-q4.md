# Q4 2024 User Interviews

## Participants
- 12 users across three segments: power users (4), new signups (5), churned users (3)

## Theme: Pricing Transparency
- 8 of 12 users mentioned wanting to see the price breakdown before the final step
- Churned users specifically cited "surprise fees" as a reason for leaving
- Power users appreciated the monthly/annual toggle but wanted clearer savings messaging

## Theme: Mobile Checkout
- 9 of 12 users attempted checkout on mobile at least once per month
- The card input fields are too small on iPhone SE and similar devices
- Auto-fill works inconsistently across browsers on mobile

## Theme: Account Creation
- Users overwhelmingly prefer guest checkout (10 of 12)
- Forced account creation at checkout is the #1 reason for cart abandonment after pricing
