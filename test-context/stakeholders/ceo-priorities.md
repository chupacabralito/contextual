# CEO Priorities -- Q1 2025

## Top 3 Priorities
1. **Launch checkout redesign by end of February** -- This is the most visible change for existing customers. Must feel faster and more trustworthy.
2. **Reduce support tickets related to billing by 40%** -- Current checkout generates ~120 tickets/month, mostly about failed payments and unclear pricing.
3. **Increase trial-to-paid conversion from 18% to 25%** -- The checkout is the last step in the funnel. Even small improvements here have outsized impact.

## Design Directives
- "I want checkout to feel like Apple. Clean, confident, no clutter."
- "Trust is everything. If users don't feel safe entering their card, nothing else matters."
- "Mobile-first. More than half our signups come from mobile now."

## Non-Negotiables
- Must support Apple Pay and Google Pay by launch
- Must work without JavaScript (progressive enhancement)
- Must pass WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility
