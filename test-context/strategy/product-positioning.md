# Contextual Pro -- Product Positioning

## Target Audience
- Solo designers and small design teams (2-5 people)
- Working on SaaS products with subscription billing
- Already using AI tools (Copilot, Claude, etc.) but not for design decisions

## Value Proposition
"Contextual gives your AI agent the same project knowledge your team has -- research findings, brand guidelines, stakeholder preferences -- so every design iteration is informed, not generic."

## Pricing Strategy
- $49/month per seat (single tier for launch)
- No free tier -- free trial only (14 days)
- Annual discount: $39/month (20% savings)

## Competitive Positioning
- vs. Figma AI: We're agent-agnostic, they're locked to their ecosystem
- vs. Manual prompting: We automate context attachment, they require copy-paste
- vs. Brand guidelines tools: We cover research + taste + strategy, not just brand
