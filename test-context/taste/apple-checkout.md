# Apple Store Checkout

## What Stands Out
- The checkout feels like a continuation of the product page, not a separate flow
- Product image stays visible throughout checkout -- constant reinforcement of what you're buying
- Payment options presented as large, tappable cards (Apple Pay, Credit Card, etc.)
- No visible "form" -- each step is a conversational prompt

## Typography
- SF Pro at 17px for body text -- larger than most checkouts
- Bold weight only for the total and CTA
- All secondary text in a lighter gray (#86868b) creating clear hierarchy

## Motion
- Steps slide in from the right, creating a sense of forward progress
- Validation animations are 200ms -- fast enough to feel instant, slow enough to notice
